# ADPS-100 Coil Winding Specifications

## For Coil Winding Service

---

## COIL 1: BIFILAR TOROIDAL PRIMARY

### Materials

- **Core:** 4× FT-240-43 ferrite toroids (stacked with epoxy)
- **Wire:** 22 AWG magnet wire, polyurethane enamel
- **Turns:** 369 (BIFILAR - two wires wound together)

### Core Dimensions (Stacked)

- Outer Diameter: 61mm
- Inner Diameter: 35.6mm
- Stack Height: 50.8mm (4 cores × 12.7mm)

### Winding Instructions

1. Stack 4 toroids, glue with epoxy, cure 2 hours
2. Prepare TWO wires, 137 meters (450 ft) each
3. Mark Wire A with RED tape, Wire B with BLUE tape
4. Hold both wires together (parallel) throughout
5. Wind 369 complete turns through center and around outside
6. Keep even tension, turns close but not overlapping

### Terminal Configuration

- **START-A (red):** Ring terminal 4mm → Goes to collector plate
- **START-B (blue):** Ring terminal 4mm → Goes to ground plane
- **CENTER TAP:** Twist END-A + END-B together, solder, ring terminal 4mm → Goes to secondary coil

### Quality Requirements

- Wire A resistance: 2-4Ω
- Wire B resistance: 2-4Ω
- A-B isolation: >10MΩ (no short between wires)
- Finish: 3 coats polyurethane varnish

## COIL 2: SECONDARY ROD COIL

### Materials

- **Core:** Ferrite rod 12.7mm diameter × 152mm length
- **Wire:** 26 AWG magnet wire, polyurethane enamel
- **Turns:** 500 (two layers of 250)

### Winding Instructions

1. Leave 13mm unwound at each end of rod
2. Winding area: 127mm (center section)
3. **Layer 1:** Wind 250 turns LEFT to RIGHT, tight and even
4. **Layer 2:** Wind 250 turns RIGHT to LEFT over layer 1
5. Apply thin varnish every 50 turns

### Terminal Configuration

- **START:** Ring terminal 4mm (red heat shrink) → Goes to bifilar center tap
- **END:** Ring terminal 4mm (black heat shrink) → Goes to rectifier input

### Quality Requirements

- DC Resistance: 15-25Ω
- Finish: 3 coats polyurethane varnish
